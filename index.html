The Delta Sigma Pi Spring 2017 Pledge Class Presents

Accelerate:

The Transit of Tomorrow

  • RSVP

Transit is changing.

Advances in technology have allowed for cars to drive without humans and trains to drive faster than airplanes. This revolution has broken the boundaries of where humans can live and travel: Hyperloops have been moving people away from urban centers while autonomous vehicles have been synergizing the reliability of public transportation with the convenience of personal cars. With cutting-edge technology from Uber, Google, Tesla, and HyperloopOne, the boundaries of transportation are being broken.

What

“Accelerate - The Transit of Tomorrow” is a UC Berkeley event open to all students targeted towards recent breakthroughs in human transportation.
